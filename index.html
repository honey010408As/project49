Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suboder
  • Changing color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts about Pegion

  1. Pegions are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pegions are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pegions have exellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  1. Panda's are omnivores
  2. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim
  3. An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day.
